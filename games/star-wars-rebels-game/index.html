Star Wars Rebels game

The Star Wars Rebels game introduces you to the new crew of the famous space shuttle, the Millennium Falcon, and its leader, Kannan Jerus. Together with them, you will once again enter the battle with the army of the Empire. By controlling the movements of the spaceship, try to destroy the maximum number of cargo ships of the imperial fleet carrying building materials to create another planet of death. The passage of the game about the star wars of the rebels will require you to have a good reaction and the ability to make decisions quickly. Help the desperate warriors of Light break the plans of the insidious Black Lord and save the starry worlds from destruction. The starship crew will be grateful for your help and will definitely invite you to their new, even more exciting adventures in the fantastic world of Star Wars.
