Wild Forest Hunter

During the Animal Hunt game you have to become a watchman who guards a small plot of land from wild animals. Try to destroy all the animals trying to get through the fence and save the entire crop. At each stage of this exciting game, you will be asked to use several types of weapons that will help protect the garden from intruders. Gold coins are awarded for each destroyed animal. It is thanks to them that you will be able to improve the characteristics of your weapons and put additional barriers in the way of the enemy. In addition, after going through several stages of the game about hunting, you will get the opportunity to play exciting mini-toys for free that require attention and good reaction.
