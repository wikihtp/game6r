Game Teenage mutant ninja turtles

Teenage mutant ninja turtles game invites you to help Michelangelo in the fight against monster robots. They are always trying to escape from their “pen”, but you need to act as quickly as possible and stop them. You will need the arrow keys for this. Press them in the correct order and then the escaped characters of the arcade game will be stopped. Be careful, because those mutant monsters that break free will certainly attack Michelangelo and try to bite him.
