Infected Wasteland

The goal of the game is to use the mouse to move a small blue square through the yellow labyrinth without touching the walls. As soon as you get to the red zones, click on it and you will be transferred to the next level of the game. But then the route will be even more confusing and narrow. The game is short and has only three levels. Once you pass them all, there will be a surprise waiting for you at the end.
